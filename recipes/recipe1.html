Bruschetta

acrisp, toasted slice of bread topped with fresh chopped tomatoes and basil, covered in balsalmic vinegar and olive oil.

ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • pinch salt
  • pinch black pepper
  • 1 tbsp fresh basil
  • 4 slices French bread

steps

  1. set oven to 450 degrees
  2. chop tomato into small cubes
  3. chop basil into short strips
  4. gently fold together Parmesan cheese, tomato, minced garlic, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, basil, salt, and pepper in a bowl
  5. brush olive oil on the bread with pastry brush
  6. put bread on baking sheet and put in oven for 5-6 mins
  7. take bread out of oven, put a generous helping of tomato mixture on top
  8. enjoy!
