Artichoke Pasta Salad

a mix of veggies, pasta, and goat cheese, with italian dressing

ingredients

    1/3 cup of the following per serving

  • uncooked orzo pasta
  • diced tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  • canned mushrooms
  • canned artichoke hearts
  • canned sliced olives
  • 1/3 cucumber

    • to taste (eyeball it)

  • feta/goat cheese
  • italian/creamy italian

steps

  1. cook the pasta according to instructions on the box
  2. dice cucumber
  3. add everything except cheese and dressing to a bowl
  4. mix contents of bowl
  5. divide into portions (if made multiple)
  6. sprinkle on cheese and drizzle italian dressing on top
  7. enjoy
