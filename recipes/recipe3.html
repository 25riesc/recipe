Banana Bread

fresh baked bread with bananas and spices in it

ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • pinch salt
  • 1 medium banana, mashed
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

steps

  1. Set oven to 350 f and grease a pan
  2. In a small bowl, combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt together
  3. Mash banana in a separate bowl
  4. Add the sugar and whip the banana and sugar together
  5. Add the melted butter, the egg yolk, and the vanilla; mix well
  6. Add the dry ingredients and mix until combined
  7. Pour into the ramekin and bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown
  8. Remove from oven, wait for it to cool to slice and enjoy
