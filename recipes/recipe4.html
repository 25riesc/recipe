Cucumber Sandwiches

cream cheese and cucumbers in between two slices of white bread

ingredients

  • 1/2 cucumber
  • loaf of sliced white bread
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tbsp cream cheese
  • dill (optional)

steps

  1. chop cucumbers into thin slices
  2. lay cucumbers on paper towels and dry them off
  3. slather cream cheese on one side of each slice of bread
  4. lay a thin layer of cucumber slices on each slice, on top of the cream cheese
  5. sprinkle on dill if desired
  6. put 2 slices together, cucumber sides together
  7. cut crusts off and cut into fourths
