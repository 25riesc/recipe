Deviled Eggs

halved hard boiled eggs with a filling of yolk and mayonaise and seasonings

ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • pinch salt and pepper, to taste
  • paprika, for garnish

steps

  1. bring a pot of water to a boil, then to a simmer
  2. put eggs in pot, boil for 14 mins
  3. put ice and water in a bowl and put eggs in the ice bath
  4. after they cool, take them out and peel them
  5. slice them in half, put yolks in a bowl
  6. add mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, salt and pepper to the bowl
  7. spoon mixture back into eggs
  8. sprinkle paprika for garnish, enjoy
